# 🌍 Climate AI Research Summaries

Summaries of papers, journal articles, and research exploring artificial
intelligence (primarily deep learning) techniques for climate and
climate-adjacent applications such as disaster prediction, pollutant tracing,
agricultural parcel segmentation, and energy grid optimization.
